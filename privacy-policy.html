Privacy Policy

How Ground Down collects, uses and protects your personal data.

Last updated: June 2025

Who We Are

Ground Down is a ground screw supply and installation company based in Lyne, Surrey. Our registered address is Lyne Lane, Lyne, Surrey KT16 0AN. You can contact us at sales@grounddown.co.uk or by phone on 07840 092397.

What Data We Collect

When you use our price calculator, send an enquiry, or contact us directly, we may collect:

  • Your name and contact details (phone number and/or email address)
  • Your site address or postcode
  • Details of your project (dimensions, screw spec, access notes)
  • Any other information you choose to share with us

We do not collect payment card data. We do not use cookies beyond basic site functionality.

How We Use Your Data

We use your data solely to:

  • Respond to your enquiry and provide a quote
  • Arrange and carry out your installation
  • Contact you about your project if needed

We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for marketing purposes without your explicit consent.

How We Store Your Data

Enquiries submitted via our website are processed by Formspree (a third-party form service). Calculator enquiries are stored securely in our CRM system (hosted by Supabase, a cloud database provider). Both services are GDPR-compliant. Your data is stored on servers within the EEA or with appropriate safeguards in place.

How Long We Keep Your Data

We retain your contact and project details for as long as necessary to fulfil your enquiry and for any follow-up. If a job is completed, we may retain records for up to 6 years for accounting and warranty purposes. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

Your Rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to or restrict how we process your data
  • Lodge a complaint with the ICO (ico.org.uk)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at sales@grounddown.co.uk.

Legal Basis for Processing

We process your data on the basis of legitimate interests (to respond to your enquiry and provide our services) and, where relevant, contractual necessity (to fulfil a job you have booked with us).

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this policy or how we handle your data, please contact us at sales@grounddown.co.uk or write to us at Lyne Lane, Lyne, Surrey KT16 0AN.
