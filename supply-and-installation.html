Standards & Workmanship

Installed to Specification, Every Time

We only supply screws that meet UK and European structural standards, and every installation follows the same process regardless of job size.

EN1090-2 Structural Steel

All screws comply with EN1090-2, the UK/European standard for steel structural components. It governs the material properties and manufacturing tolerances that make a ground screw genuinely load-bearing.

EN ISO 1461 Hot-Dip Galvanizing

Every screw is hot-dip galvanised to EN ISO 1461 — the same coating standard used in civil engineering. A minimum 85µm zinc layer designed for decades of service in UK ground conditions.

Radar Scanning on Every Job

We scan for underground services before installation on every job. If an obstruction prevents a screw being sunk to depth, there's no charge for that position — no negotiation needed.
