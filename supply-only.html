Is Supply Only Right for You?

Supply only is the right choice for a specific type of customer. Read below to see if it applies to you.

Experienced Trade Installers

If you're already competent in ground screw installation, have access to appropriate machinery and understand the installation requirements — supply only is available to you.

Contractors with Own Equipment

Trade contractors who carry their own ground screw installation machinery can purchase our screws independently. Contact us for trade pricing.

Repeat Trade Customers

Regular trade accounts are available for contractors who need a reliable, consistent supply of ground screws for ongoing projects.

Not sure which option is right for you? If you're a homeowner, a garden room company or an installer without ground screw installation experience, our supply and installation service is the better choice. We handle everything — the screws and the installation.
The Product

Four Lengths. One Quality Standard.

We supply ground screws in four lengths — 1m, 1.25m, 1.5m and 2m. For most garden room foundations, the 1.25m is the right choice. Other lengths are available when the site, structure or ground conditions call for something different. All four are the same product quality we use in our own installations.

Proven across all South East England ground conditions

Clay, loam, sandy soils — our screws have been installed across hundreds of sites. They perform consistently regardless of ground type.

The right size for the job

Our 1.25m screw is the standard choice for most garden room foundations. Shallower sites may suit the 1m; deeper ground, steeper slopes or heavier structures can call for our 1.5m or 2m lengths.

The same screws we use ourselves

We supply exactly what we install — not a different product grade. What you buy is what we'd put in the ground ourselves.
